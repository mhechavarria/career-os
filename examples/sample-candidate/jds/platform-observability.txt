Lumen Cloud — Platform Engineer, Observability (Remote)

About the role
Lumen Cloud runs a multi-tenant platform and we are investing heavily in observability. We
are looking for a Platform Engineer to own the metrics, tracing, and logging stack and to
make it effortless for product teams to operate their services. You will build paved-road
tooling on Kubernetes and AWS and partner closely with our SRE function.

What you will do
- Own and scale the observability stack: Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, and Datadog.
- Integrate error tracking with Sentry across all services.
- Build self-service dashboards, SLOs, and alerting for product teams.
- Provision platform infrastructure on Kubernetes (AWS) with Terraform and Helm.
- Automate configuration and host management with Ansible.
- Maintain CI/CD pipelines and Docker-based build tooling.
- Write platform tooling and exporters in Go.

What we are looking for
- Deep experience with Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, and Datadog.
- Production experience with Kubernetes, AWS, Terraform, Helm, and Ansible.
- An SRE mindset: SLOs, error budgets, structured logging, and on-call health.
- Proficiency in Go and a strong sense of developer experience.

Nice to have
- Experience with Sentry and distributed tracing at scale.
- Cost-optimization work driven by tracing and metrics.
