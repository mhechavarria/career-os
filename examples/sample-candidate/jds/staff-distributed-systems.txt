Quasar — Staff Backend Engineer, Distributed Systems (Remote)

About the role
Quasar processes billions of events per day for a real-time risk platform. We are hiring a
Staff Backend Engineer to set technical direction for our distributed systems: the
event-driven core, the event sourcing model, and the high availability story that keeps it
running. This is a hands-on staff role — you will write code, lead system design, and mentor.

What you will do
- Lead system design for event-driven services built around Apache Kafka and PostgreSQL.
- Evolve our event sourcing architecture and the projections built on top of it.
- Drive high availability and disaster-recovery design across multiple AWS regions.
- Set standards for gRPC service contracts and backward compatibility.
- Run workloads on Kubernetes, provisioned with Terraform, deployed with Helm.
- Use Ansible for configuration management of supporting infrastructure.
- Champion observability with Datadog, Prometheus, and OpenTelemetry.

What we are looking for
- Extensive experience designing distributed systems at scale.
- Mastery of Apache Kafka, event-driven architecture, and event sourcing.
- Strong PostgreSQL and data-modelling skills under high availability constraints.
- Production experience with Kubernetes, AWS, Terraform, Helm, and Ansible.
- Fluency in Go and TypeScript; excellent system design judgment.

Nice to have
- Experience authoring ADRs and leading design reviews.
- Background spanning embedded or IoT through to cloud.
