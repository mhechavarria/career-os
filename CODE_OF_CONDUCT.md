# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in Career OS to be a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind, respectful, and considerate toward others.
- Welcoming differing viewpoints and accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Behavior that will not be tolerated includes harassment of any kind, personal
attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and any
conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional
setting.

## Our standard, by reference

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant][covenant], version 2.1. Please
read the full text for the complete set of standards, responsibilities, and
enforcement guidelines:

- <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior can be
reported privately to the maintainers through GitHub's
[report abuse](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam)
feature, or for non-sensitive matters by opening an [issue](../../issues). All
reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the
maintainers will respect the privacy and security of anyone who reports an
incident.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
discussions, and code) and when an individual is representing the project in
public spaces.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org
